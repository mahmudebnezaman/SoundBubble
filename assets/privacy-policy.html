👋 Introduction

Welcome to SoundBubble, developed by Codezam Labs (com.codezamlabs.soundbubble). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being fully transparent about our data practices.

🔒 Privacy at a Glance

SoundBubble is a fully offline application. We do not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data. All settings are stored exclusively on your device.
  • No personal data collected — ever
  • No internet access — the app has no network permissions
  • No third-party analytics — no Firebase, AdMob, or tracking SDKs
  • No account required — use it anonymously
  • All settings stay on your device — never uploaded anywhere

🛡️ Permissions Explained

SoundBubble requests only the permissions necessary to provide its floating volume-control functionality. Below is a plain-language explanation of each permission.

Permission Why It's Needed
Draw Over Other Apps
SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW		 Displays the floating bubble overlay on top of other apps. Without this, the core feature cannot work.
Foreground Service
FOREGROUND_SERVICE		 Keeps the bubble active and responsive while you use other apps. Required by Android for persistent overlays.
Modify Audio Settings
MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS		 Reads and adjusts volume levels for Ring, Media, Alarm, Call, and Notification streams — the app's primary function.
Post Notifications
POST_NOTIFICATIONS		 Shows a persistent notification required by Android when a foreground service is running (Android 13+).
Receive Boot Completed
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED		 Optionally restarts the bubble service after device reboot, if you have enabled that setting.
Notification Policy Access
ACCESS_NOTIFICATION_POLICY		 Allows toggling between Ring, Vibrate, and Silent ringer modes, including Do Not Disturb management.
✅

None of these permissions are used to collect, read, or transmit personal data. All permission usage is strictly limited to controlling on-device audio settings and displaying the floating widget.

💾 Data Stored on Your Device

SoundBubble saves the following preference data locally on your device using Android DataStore. This data never leaves your device.

  • Bubble appearance — opacity, size, and color you selected
  • Bubble position — last X/Y coordinates on screen
  • Service state — whether the bubble service is enabled
  • Ringer mode — your last selected ringer mode (Ring/Vibrate/Silent)

You can clear all app data at any time via Settings → Apps → SoundBubble → Clear Data on your Android device.

🚫 Data We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do not collect, access, or process:

  • Name, email address, or any personally identifiable information
  • Phone number or device identifiers (IMEI, Advertising ID)
  • Location data (GPS or network-based)
  • Contacts, call logs, or SMS messages
  • Photos, media files, or storage contents
  • Audio recordings or microphone input
  • Browsing history or clipboard contents
  • Usage statistics or crash reports
  • Financial or payment information

🔌 Third-Party Services

SoundBubble does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or data collection services. The app operates entirely offline using only Android system APIs and first-party AndroidX libraries.

✅

No Firebase · No AdMob · No Crashlytics · No Facebook SDK · No Amplitude · No Mixpanel · No third-party tracking of any kind.
